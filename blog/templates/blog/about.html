About The Blog Who Lived

BY Harikrishnan A P • This page lives to tell the tale

Welcome to The Blog Who Lived — a developer’s journal built on code, caffeine, and second chances.

The Blog Who Lived isn’t just a blog — it’s a space for stories that deserve to be heard. Whether it’s code you wrote, a memory you carry, a lesson life taught you, or something the world needs to know — this is where it lives.

The name? It’s a quiet rebellion — against fear, delay, and doubt. This blog lived through it all. Yeah, you guessed it — a Harry Potter fanboy.

So write that post. About your first project. Or your worst heartbreak. Or your favorite food. If it mattered to you, it belongs here. You matter. Your story matters. We're listening.

Whether you're here to learn, write, or just scroll — thanks for stopping by.
